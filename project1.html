H2R UX Research Case Study

As a club member, I wanted to improve the experience of fellow members by making information easily accessible and quickly available.

Members are unable to get timely updates and alerts. They do not have easy access to race calendar and race results, and unaware when other members have auto parts for sale/trade.

I created "Personas" to identify my users, which in turn enabled me to come up with a design.
